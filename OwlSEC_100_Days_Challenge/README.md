# OwlSEC 100 Days of Hacking Challenge
RadicalEds Progress Log Archive (Originally Formatted For Discord)

### What Is It?
The OwlSEC Community Has Issued a Challenge to Its Members.   
Work on Something Once a day and submit a Progress Report, With Proof, Each and Every day for 100 days.   

### When Is It?
The Event Began on November 1st, 2025

### Where Is It?
On Discord, within the OwlSEC Community    
Members who wanted to participate, needed to claim the role by reacting to the announcement   
Having the Role Gives Access to both the 100days chat channel, and the 100days Forum Channel where users post their progress within their own thread.

### Why Is It?
The Purpose of the Event is to build healthy habbits and to Challenge thy Self.    
There Are Rewards for Completing the Challenge.
